Highlands Prime Steakhouse


If you're looking for a steakhouse in MOA, then look no further! At Highlands Prime Steakhouse,
you can get steaks in different cuts from rib eye to filet mignon.
In fact, their steaks are the highlight of the menu since they're in their own section. Note: They're closed for now.
2/F Entertainment Mall, North Wing, SM Mall of Asia, Pasay

 
 
 

Racks


Although they're known for offering stellar racks of ribs, one shouldn't turn a bline eye to Racks' steaks.
They offer US rib eye steaks that are rich, tender, full-flavored,
and have generous marbling. They even offer a salmon steak to those who aren't fans of meat.
G/F SM Mall of Asia, Entertainment Mall, Seaside Blvd, Manila Bay, Reclamation Area, Pasay
(02) 556-4786 to 87

 
 
 

Tomahawk Chops and Grill


Want the ultimate fusion of Homegrown Cowboy and Native American flavors?
At Tomahawk Chops and Grill, you can avail of their famous Tomahawk steak, Tomahawk chops,
steaks, fish, and chicken all paired with their "dirty" rice.

2/F SM Mall of Asia, South Veranda, Seaside Blvd, Pasay
